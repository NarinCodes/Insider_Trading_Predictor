House Stockwatcher: Identify possible instances of Insider Trading

by: Narin Dhatwalia

This app allows users to predict the profitability of stock investments made by members of U.S. Congress. The underlying model provides a probability score to quantify whether an investment made by a U.S. lawmaker will be profitable in the near future. Results from this app can benefit retail investors while picking their own stocks, as well as watchdog groups to investigate possible cases of insider trading.
